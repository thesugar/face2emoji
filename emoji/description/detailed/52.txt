face with tears of joy. A yellow face with a big grin, uplifted eyebrows, and smiling eyes, each shedding a tear from laughing so hard. Widely used to show something is funny or pleasing. Not to be confused with the sad Loudly Crying Face. One of the ten most popular emojis between 2014–2018 and was named the Oxford Dictionaries 2015 “Word of the Year”.
